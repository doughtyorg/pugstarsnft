The Pugstar Saga

They're cute, charming and full of personality that's out of this world - So, it's no wonder the Pugstars are not actually from this planet. The Pugstars inhabited a planet filled with advanced technology and wonder, but left to seek out new realms and be worshiped as the very gods they believed themselves to be (all while spreading their cuteness)!

But being locked up in a ship is no life for a dog and after an unfortunate accident where a navigation system was mistaken for a fire hydrant, the ship spent decades adrift... (that's even longer in dog-years!) until they crash landed on Earth. But once they opened their hatch and stepped onto our soil, did planetary domination ensue?

No. You see, the Pugstars had simply forgot their mission. Maybe it was all that new oxygen in the fresh air, or something about the smell of bacon or their occassional lazy eye that made them lose sight of their mission. Or was or all those years in space? Noone really knows what lowered their IQs - They don't even remember how to read their own history books!

Either way, two few things are certain for the Pugstars on Earth:

  1. 1) The extra gravity here makes the extra rolls in their skin show (they really look much more svelte on their homeplanet), and
  2. 2) Once adopted, every unique Pugstar will become the center of its household, drawing all the attention, admiration, praise, love and treats they could ever want or need from their caregivers.

...Hey, you know what? Maybe they did succeed in their mission to be worshiped like gods...

After all, it really is a dog's life.
